Capabilities

WHAT IT DOES

A complete fitness evaluation engine, powered by computer vision and AI.

🦴
33-Point Pose Tracking

MediaPipe detects every major joint — shoulders, elbows, hips, knees, ankles — and tracks them across every frame of your video.

🔢
Automatic Rep Counting

Joint angles are analysed to detect the full up-down cycle of each exercise type. No manual input needed.

⭐
Form Score (0–100%)

Every rep is graded on correctness. Partial reps, incorrect depth, and compensatory movements all reduce your score.

🛡️
Cheat Detection

The AI flags suspicious patterns — hidden face, speed spikes, confidence drops — and marks the test for admin review.

📊
Benchmarks & Percentiles

Your result is compared against beginner, intermediate and advanced benchmarks for that exercise. You get a form grade (A–F), a fitness level, and an estimated percentile — so you always know exactly where you stand.
